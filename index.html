Our Theme

Our Heritage & Black-Tie Spring Wedding celebrates the timeless elegance of formal black tie with a vibrant African cultural twist.

The light and refreshing colour palette blends classic black and white with soft spring hues like Blush Pink, Lavender, Peach, Chartreuse Yellow, Mint, or Icy Blue, accented with luxurious metallics like Gold, Silver, and Bronze.

Ideally, men can complement classic black suits and tuxedos with pastel-toned pocket squares or ties, while women can opt for sweeping gowns or dresses in the aforementioned soft hues.

Guests are also encouraged to wear and incorporate traditional African attire into the theme, using rich fabrics and patterns that reflect these colours.

Think regal silhouettes, delicate embroidery, and bold accessories that honour heritage whilst embracing the sophistication of a black-tie event to create a wedding that is both modern and deeply meaningful.

Our Love Story

It all began with a simple online message—a message that would unknowingly set the stage for a love story that would change both of our lives forever. From the first “Hello,” the conversation flowed effortlessly, as though we had been waiting our whole lives to find someone who truly understood us.

In the days that followed, our conversations grew longer, more meaningful, filled with laughter, shared dreams, and stories of our similar upbringings. Our shared faith and deeply rooted family values became the foundation of a bond that felt divinely orchestrated.

At the time, both of us lived and worked in London, a coincidence that felt anything but accidental. London Waterloo Station became our special meeting point—a place where our friendship blossomed amidst the hum of the city and the rush of commuters. For three wonderful months, we spent countless evenings walking by the Thames, grabbing coffee, and talking until time slipped away unnoticed

It was during one of those simple yet magical evenings that Jon took a brave step, expressing his desire for something more than friendship. With hearts open and hopeful, we took the leap together, embarking on a journey that would be as intentional as it was beautiful.

Even when distance temporarily separated us, our commitment never wavered. We made it a priority to create meaningful memories despite the miles. Meeting each other’s closest friends and families became a cherished part of our story, each introduction drawing us closer together. Natalie’s heart was warmed by Jon’s father, whose warm approval cemented what we already knew—we were meant to be.

Jon’s move out of London to be nearer to Natalie marked a turning point in our relationship. In just four months, Jon had seamlessly woven himself into Natalie’s family life, becoming a beloved part of their world. Our prayers and shared pursuit of Godly counsel deepened our love and strengthened our convictions as we officially began our courtship

Six months later, Jon knelt before Natalie in a private, intimate setting, eyes filled with certainty and love, promising a future built on trust, faith, and unending devotion. With tears of joy, Natalie said “Yes,” knowing without a doubt that this was the beginning of forever.

The rest, as they say, is history—a history filled with love, laughter, and unwavering faith. Now, as we prepare to celebrate this next chapter, we look back with gratitude and forward with excitement, knowing that the best is yet to come.
