Our Colour Scheme

Black-Tie Spring Wedding

The light and refreshing colour palette blends classic black and white with soft spring hues like Blush Pink, Lavender, Peach, Chartreuse Yellow, Mint, or Icy Blue, accented with luxurious metallics like Gold, Silver, and Bronze.

Guests are also encouraged to wear and incorporate traditional African attire into the theme, using rich fabrics and patterns that reflect these colours.

Our Love Story

It all started with a simple message that sparked something truly special. From that moment, long and frequent conversations filled our days and nights over the next few weeks. We quickly discovered just how much we had in common—our similar upbringing, shared faith, and deeply rooted family values created an instant connection.

At the time, we were both working in London, which gave us the opportunity to meet regularly after work at London Waterloo Station. Over the course of three months, we built a strong and genuine friendship. It was during this time that Jon expressed his desire to pursue something more, and we began a beautiful journey together.

Even when distance came into play, we made it a priority to create meaningful memories. Introducing each other to our close friends and families brought us even closer as we prayed and sought Godly counsel along the way, strengthening our convictions. Natalie met Jon’s father, who was very pleased with Jon’s choice. Soon after, Jon moved out of London to be closer to Natalie and after just four months, Jon had seamlessly become part of the family, and our courtship officially began.

Six months after that, Jon proposed to Natalie in a private and intimate moment, making a promise to build a life together. The rest, as they say, is history—and now, we can’t wait to celebrate the next chapter of our story with all of you.
